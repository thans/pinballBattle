Pinball Battle

A multiplayer, FFA, Pinball "RTS" game

Andreas Sjöroos | Anton Warnhag | Sebastian Münzner | Ting-Yao Fam | Tore Hanssen

Pinball
like you have never seen before

Multiplayer pinball with free-for-all action! A couch friendly strategy game based on broad stroke army control. Move your army down the battle field and destroy the enemy's keep - but watch out for the pinball, acid pits, and enemy bumper towers!

Coming soon to the console in your living room!

Conceived and Developed at KTH

This project was developed through DH2650 - Computer Game Design at KTH in Stockholm, Sweden during Autumn, 2014. The team? Andreas Sjöroos, Anton Warnhag, Sebastian Münzner, Ting-Yao Fam, and Tore Hanssen

Developed in Unity

Pinball Battle was developed in Unity using C#. Most graphical assets were borrowed from the Unity Asset Store or from free 3d models found online, but some were created using blender.
